About Me

My name is Stephen Afam-Osemene and i studied Mechanical Engineering at the University of Benin. I am a big fan of technology, i love it a lot ; i love its ability to improve lives and change the situations around us.Technology has both its good and bad but we have to take responsibility so that the good outways the bad.

I started my journey in Software Development because i was trying to make impact; i needed a website at the time and i couldnt afford it, so i decided to do it myself and that has been the best decision of my life. Software feels limitless, the feel of creating something from absolutely nothing; taking a blank canvas and producing solutions “wow” that is extremely alluring.

I am good at what i do not because of what i know but because of cravings for more knowledge, my ability to understand fast and evolve; this is my most important attribute, my most valuable attribute.
